Frequently Asked Questions

Can I set up recurrent sessions?

Yes! By registering an account, you are able to set up recurring bookings.
We will notify you 72 hours before your booking of its status via email or phone,
and can cancel your appointment 24 hours before booking time without fee.

Can I choose a specific trainer?

Yes you can! When you make a booking, under 'choose trainer' you can pick
The trainer that most adequately fits your needs. More information on them in about us.

How do I check my bookings?

Within your user profile, you can check your current bookings and past bookings
With all available information, such as time, trainer, and type of training.
