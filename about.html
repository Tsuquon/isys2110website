Meet the Trainers

We are a team of professional dog trainers that have a passion for nurturing and training human's most beloved pet.

Bob Vance

Hey there! I'm Bob Vance, your enthusiastic and dedicated dog trainer, ready to make your furry friend's exercise routine a blast! I absolutely adore dogs and have a ton of experience in keeping them active, happy, and healthy

What I offer:

  1. Splish-Splash Swimming: I specialize in water adventures for water-loving pups. Whether it's boosting confidence or giving them a low-impact workout, I make sure they have a paw-some time while staying safe.
  2. Frisbee Frenzy: Get ready for some high-flying fun! I'm an expert at teaching dogs to catch and retrieve frisbees, creating an unbreakable bond between you and your four-legged buddy.
  3. Wag-tastic Walks: From leisurely strolls to energizing power walks, I customize each outing to suit your dog's needs. It's all about exercise, socializing, and keeping those tails wagging!
  4. Fetch Fiesta: Let's level up your dog's fetch game! We'll work on precision, timing, and endless fun with toys, balls, and sticks. Get ready for some serious tail-chasing action!
  5. Running Adventures: Got a high-energy pup? No problem! I offer exhilarating runs that will leave your dog panting and satisfied. We'll tailor the distance and pace to match their capabilities.
  6. Social Playtime: Dogs love to make friends, and I organize engaging group activities to foster positive play and socialization. It's a great way for your furry friend to have a barking good time!
  7. Zen Dog Yoga: Unleash your pup's inner zen! As a certified dog yoga instructor, I'll guide your furry friend through gentle stretches and relaxation exercises. It's all about finding balance and harmony.

My Approach:

I believe in the power of positive reinforcement, making training a tail-wagging experience for your pup. Safety, well-being, and loads of fun are my top priorities. I'll create a nurturing environment where your dog can thrive and be their best.

Credentials and Experience:

  • 12 of dog training and exercise experience
  • Certified in various exercise techniques
  • Extensive knowledge of dog behavior, health, and safety
  • Happy clients and satisfied wagging tails

I'm beyond excited to meet you and your furry companion! Together, we'll create an exercise routine that will keep your dog jumping for joy. Let's make tails wag and adventures happen!
